[h1]爱梅斯、真步（探险家）、镜华（夏日戏水）领导的兰德索尔：先睹为快[/h1]

使用全新的领袖进行统治吧——本模组包含了3位《公主连结 Re:Dive》游戏发布4.5周年之际发布的角色，为领导兰德索尔的玩家提供更多不同的统治方式。

[b]爱梅斯[/b]的特色能力是“[b]重塑之羽[/b]”。这项能力使其能够重塑阵亡的高级单位，并在游戏开始时免费获得信仰值。一旦建造圣地，他们可以立即获得大预言家并随后获得一个强力的战斗单位“[b]可可萝[/b]”。可可萝有独特的升级树且可以撤退回首都，但在一局游戏中只能获得一次。

公主骑士为她提供的能力是“[b]繁花之幸[/b]”。研发新时代的首个科技或市政后获得1个该时代的尤里卡和鼓舞。处于黑暗时代时，尤里卡和鼓舞可立即完成科技或市政。爱梅斯可以在建有公会之家的城市开展“入梦”特色项目，这一项目将为他们提供记忆碎片巨作，提供给他们大量额外产出。

[b]真步（探险家）[/b]的特色能力是“[b]真步真步冒险团[/b]”。侦察单位的生产更加快速，且在新大陆上获得额外的战斗力。有了这一特性，他们将能够更加快速地探索世界。特色建筑[b]真步真步营地[/b]使棉花和染料提供额外文化值，在新大陆上还提供额外的忠诚度和金币。

“[b]童话探险[/b]”是公主骑士提供给探险家真步的额外能力。外国文明的每个代表团或大使馆均为真步提供文化值。在一片新大陆上，他们的单位行动更加迅速，建立第一座城市时还可以免费获得开拓者和侦察单位。

[b]镜华（夏日戏水）[/b]的特色能力是“[b]水跃浪峰[/b]”，这项能力使得他们在海边能够占据优势。相邻或位于海岸或湖泊的改良设施产出随机数额的额外科技值、生产力或文化值。

与她的领袖能力相配合的是公主骑士提供的额外能力“[b]夏日水弹群[/b]”。在沿海地区建设区域、建筑和改良设施便可为城市带来可观的科技值、生产力和文化值加成。同时，远程单位的攻击可以降低目标单位的战斗力。

您将会如何使用这些全新或是经过更新的领袖？您将会如何带领兰德索尔建立经得起时间考验的文明？

[h1]模组特性：[/h1]

[b]强力而不超模。[/b]每位领袖的能力均鼓励一种独特的游玩方式，并在设计时充分考虑了领袖的能力强度，使其拥有充分的竞争力但并不显著过强。

[b]忠于原作。[/b]本作品在制作过程中进行了精细的考证，包括人名、地名、城市名称和外交用语，以尽最大可能表现原作的风土人情和领袖性格特征。

[b]和谐风格。[/b]所有已制作的美术素材均参照《文明6》游戏的规格进行处理，确保其在各个界面正确显示且整洁清爽。

[b]领袖语音。[/b]她们均有与游戏内文本相符合的，提取自原作游戏的语音。

[h1]Ameth, Maho (Explorer) and Kyoka (Summertime) Lead Landosol: First Look[/h1]

Rule with brand new leaders - this mod contains 3 characters released near the 4.5 years anniversary of mobile game Princess Connect Re:Dive, providing more different ways to rule for players leading Landosol.

[b]Ameth[/b]'s unique ability is [b]Rifàre Piuma[/b] (literally "Reshap(ing) Feather(s)"). [i](Note: This name might be a mistranslation by the original game of "reshaping wings" since the word for "wing" in Japanese can also be interpreted as "feather", and the Italian words they used seem to be grammatically incorrect. We used the name as is for now.)[/i] This ability allows them to retrain units with high levels when they are killed, and grants free Faith when game starts. Once they build a Holy Site, they can gain a Great Prophet at once and later receive a [b]Kokkoro[/b], a powerful combat unit which has a unique promotion tree and can retreat to capital but can only be acquired once per game.

Princess Knight grants her [b]Fiore Felicità[/b] (literally "Flower Happiness"). When the first technology or civic of a new Era is researched, earn an Eureka and Inspiration of that Era. When in a Dark Age, Eurekas and Inspirations can finish research of technologies or civics instantly. Ameth can initiate Enter Dream World unique project in cities having a Guildhouse, which grant Memory Piece great works that provide much extra yields for them.

Unique ability of [b]Maho (Explorer)[/b] is [b]Maho Maho Adventurers[/b]. They train recon units faster and recon units have bonus Combat Strength on a new continent. With this ability, they can explore the world faster. Unique building [b]Maho Maho Camp[/b] allows Cotton and Dyes in the city it is in gain extra Culture, and provides extra Loyalty and Gold in new continents.

[b]Fairytale Exploration[/b] is the unique ability that Princess Knight provides for Explorer Maho. Each Delegation or Embassy from a foreign civilization provides extra Culture for Explorer Maho. On a new continent, their units move faster, and they can receive a free Settler and a recon unit when they found the first city.

[b]Kyoka (Summertime)[/b] has a unique ability named [b]Aqua Crest[/b] which provides advantages when they are near Coasts. Each improvement on or next to Coast or Lake tiles provides random amounts of extra Science, Production or Culture.

The extra ability that Princess Knight provides in synergy with her leader ability is [b]Summer Aqua Flock[/b]. Constructing districts, buildings and improvements in coastal areas provides considerable bonus Science, Production and Culture for their cities. In addition, ranged attacks of ranged units can reduce Combat Strength to the target unit.

How will you use these brand new or updated leaders? How will you lead Landosol to build a civilization that stands the test of time?

[h1]Mod Highlights:[/h1]

[b]"Powered" without "Over"[/b]: Each Landosol leader has several abilities that promote a unique play-style. This mod is designed to make abilities of all leaders included competent but not overpowered.

[b]Lore-Loyalty[/b]: This mod is made with a fine research of the original franchise, and provides full lists of named places, citizen names, city names and diplomacy statements to better represent lore and leader characteristics.

[b]Harmonized Mix[/b]: All art assets included in this mod are processed to fit the need of Civilization VI, so that they can display correctly and neatly.

[b]Voiced Leader[/b]: All of new leaders have speech audio extracted from the original game which corresponds to in-game text.

[h1]Known issues:[/h1]

Some Civilopedia articles are in Simplified Chinese only.

Names of Ameth's traits are direct restorations of the original Japanese transcriptions of Italian words which might be grammatically incorrect. None of authors of this mod can speak Italian, so we might need some support to set better ability names.
